pass_stadium

The map pass_stadium was created by Exer for the Team Fortress 2 4v4 PASS Time community. It has gone through many revisions since its initial conception, reported by Exer to be due to not being as skilled as he is now at 4v4 PASS Time. This map has grown with him.
