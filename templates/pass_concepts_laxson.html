pass_concepts

The maps created by Laxson for the Team Fortress 2 4v4 PASS Time community. These are all rough concept maps meant to explore what other variants of PASS Time could look like.

pass_waterpolo - the entire map is flooded partially; inspired waterpolo2
pass_basketball_a1 - looks like v2 of pass_basic with basketball hoops instead of goals and a top spawner
pass_basic - prototype passtime map with basic functionality
pass_volleyball - volleyball with the jack. likely used modified throw distances. assuming this is laxson's
pass_valley_v1 - variant with mid being a valley and water and side ramps
pass_skate_a1 - variant with just one middle ramp and high goals
pass_rugby - variant with just two side ramps
pass_mix - pass_football, changed and colored geometry, likely v5
pass_football2 - pass_football, likely v4
pass_football - working but unfinished, likely v3
passtime_soccer_big - more finished but still unfinished, huge skybox, likely v2
passtime_soccer - totally unfinished, non-working, likely v1
